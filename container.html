GANESHA

Vinayaka is another name for Lord Ganesha, a revered Hindu deity known as the remover of obstacles and the god of wisdom and new beginnings. He is recognized by his elephant head

VENKATESWARA SWAMY

Venkateswara Swamy, also known as Lord Balaji, is an incarnation of Lord Vishnu who resides at the famous Tirumala Temple, a significant pilgrimage site in India.

DURGAMMA

Durga is a revered Hindu goddess symbolizing strength, motherhood, and the ultimate feminine power (Shakti), known for her victory over the demon Mahishasura and her role in upholding cosmic order

MAHADEV

Shiva is a principal deity in Hinduism, representing the destroyer and transformer within the Hindu trinity, which also includes Brahma (the creator) and Vishnu (the preserver). Known by names like Mahadeva and Mahakal, Shiva is the god of destruction who, through this cycle of destruction and renewal, maintains the life cycle of the universe.

HANUMAN

Hanuman is a revered Hindu deity known for his incredible strength, unwavering devotion to Lord Rama, and his role in the Ramayana, where he helped Rama rescue Sita from Ravana. A divine Vanara, he is considered an immortal being (Chiranjeevi) and a symbol of courage, perseverance, and selfless service
